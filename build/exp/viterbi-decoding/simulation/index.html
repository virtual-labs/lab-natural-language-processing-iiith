Instructions

  • Select a corpus from the dropdown menu. By default, 'Corpus A' is selected and loaded.
  • Observe the displayed emission and transition matrices, which are computed from the training sentence.
  • In the Viterbi table, enter the probability values for each POS tag and word in the test sentence. All columns are available for input at once.
  • Click the "Check" button to validate your answers. Correct values are retained; incorrect ones can be revised. Feedback is provided for each attempt.
  • If you need help, click "Show Hint" for guidance. To see the correct answers and compare them with your input, click "Show Answer" (only after entering your answers).
  • Once all answers are correct, the simulation displays the decoded POS tags for the test sentence.
  • To start over or switch to a different corpus, use the "Reset" button (which always resets to Corpus A) or select a new corpus from the dropdown.
